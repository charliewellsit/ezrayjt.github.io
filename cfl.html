Compact Fluorescent Lamp (CFL)


Compact Fluorescent Lamp (CFL) are designed with a bent or folded tube to fit standard light fixtures and include a compact electronic ballast in their base.

They consume only a fraction of the electricity compared to incandescent bulbs whilst offering an eight to fifteen times longer lifespan.

Despite their higher initial price, CFL can save more than five times their purchase cost throughout their usage.

However, given the toxic mercury content, CFL require proper disposal in many countries.
