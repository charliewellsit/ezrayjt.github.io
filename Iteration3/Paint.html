Paint

  • Whenever feasible, try to repurpose paints to minimize their impact on the environment, given their toxic nature.
  • Pre-1970s homes often feature lead-based paint, posing potential health risks. It's essential to handle and dispose of these lead-contaminated materials properly. Prior to commencing any renovation or construction work, reach out to your local concil for guidance on appropriate disposal procedures. .
