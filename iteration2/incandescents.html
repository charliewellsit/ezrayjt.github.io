Incandescent Lamp

Incandescent light bulbs use a heated filament in a glass bulb, but they're highly inefficient, converting less than 5% of energy into light.

They produce heat and have short lifespans compared to alternatives like fluorescent and LED lights, leading to government-led phase-outs for energy conservation.
