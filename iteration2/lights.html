Ways To Improve Your Room's Lighting Level



There are several tips to enhance the brightness of your room!


Consider the colour of the lightbulb


Lightbulbs with higher color temperatures (e.g., 5000K or higher) emit cooler, bluish-white light that can make your room appear brighter whilst lower color temperatures (e.g., 2700K) produce warmer, yellowish light that creates a cozy and less intense ambiance, making the room seem dimmer.

User mirrors to reflect lights


By reflecting and dispersing natural and artificial light sources with the use of mirrors, you can enhance the room's brightness. This can be done by placing mirrors opposite windows or light fixtures which can bounce light across the space, making your room appear brighter.

Opt for lighter wall colours


Lighter wall colours can significantly impact a room's brightness by reflecting more natural and artificial light. The lighter color of the wallpaper helps to distribute and amplify the available light, making the space feel more spacious and well-lit.
